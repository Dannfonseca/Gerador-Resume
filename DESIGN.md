## Overview
Architectural Minimalism meets Journalistic Gravitas. The UI evokes a premium matte finish — a high-end broadsheet or contemporary gallery. No glassmorphism, no borders rounded. Pure matte shapes.

## Colors
The palette is rooted in high-contrast neutrals and a single accent color.
- **Primary (#1A1C1E):** Deep ink for headlines and core text.
- **Secondary (#6C7278):** Sophisticated slate for borders, captions, metadata.
- **Tertiary (#B8422E):** "Boston Clay" — the sole driver for interaction.
- **Neutral (#F7F5F2):** Warm limestone foundation, softer than pure white.

## Typography
- **Public Sans:** Used for all primary reading text and large, impactful headlines. It gives a broadsheet gravitas.
- **Space Grotesk:** Used for technical labels, buttons, navigation items. It gives an architectural blueprint feel.

## Forms & Interactions
Buttons and inputs have zero border-radius. They are sharp, definitive, and matte. Interactions rely on Boston Clay (`#B8422E`) to draw the eye exclusively to primary actions.
